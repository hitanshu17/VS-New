Precision Accounting for Grading & Sorting

Whether you're handling fruits, vegetables, spices, or grains; grading and packaging play a key role in product value. Our software automates the physical workflow and connects it with your inventory, billing, and financial accounting.

Batch Tracking

Raw material is logged upon arrival with unique batch codes linked to vendor, date, and input quantity for full traceability.

Quality Sorting

Sort products by grade, size, type with auto price-mapping for each category and quantity recording per grade.

Packaging Line

Monitor pack-wise output (box, bag, tray), track wastage and leftover stock, calculate cost per packaging unit.

Inventory Auto-Update

Stock updated grade-wise and pack-wise with real-time inventory by godown or store, tracking shelf life and expiry.

Sales Integration

Directly dispatch packed goods with gate pass and delivery details, bill generation linked to stock and grade.

Ledger Posting

Purchases and sales automatically reflect in ledgers with payment, receipt, and journal vouchers.
