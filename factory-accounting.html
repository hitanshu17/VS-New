End-to-End Process Flow

Track every movement from raw input to revenue output. Our system connects every step so you stay in complete control.

1. Purchase Made Simple

Log every entry from vendors, with or without a bill, and sync it with your Item Master (MST) which holds all material specs.

01

2. Seamless Production

Material moves to Production for grading, processing, and packing, controlled by the Factory MST to ensure quality standards.

02

3. Real-Time Stock Management

Finished goods are added to stock. The system tracks quantities and locations in real-time, ready for sale or dispatch.

03

4. Smart Gate Pass System

A digitally logged Gate Pass is issued when products move, defining if they are for sale or for further processing.

04

5. Instant Billing

Based on demand and linked to the Buyer MST, the system auto-generates bills with taxes, discounts, and shipment details.

05

6. Auto Ledger Updates

Every bill is posted directly to the ledger, creating necessary vouchers and giving you a complete, traceable financial trail in real time.
