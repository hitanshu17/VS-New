Paper GSM & Layer Configuration

Our software provides precise control over paper grades and weights, allowing you to configure every layer of your corrugated boards with GSM accuracy.

Choose from Kraft, Duplex, Testliner and other paper types, define the GSM for each layer (e.g., 120/150/180), and configure ply structures (3-ply, 5-ply, 7-ply) to meet exact customer specifications.
